44th President of the United States

Barack Obama served two terms as president, from 2009 to 2017. He was the first African-American president in U.S. history. Barack Hussein Obama II is an American politician, lawyer, and author who served as the 44th president of the United States from 2009 to 2017. A member of the Democratic Party, Obama was the first African-American president of the United States. President Obama served 2 terms in office and lives in his native Illinois.
